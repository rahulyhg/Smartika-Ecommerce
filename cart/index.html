Products you add to Wishlist will be listed below.

A Wishlist is simply products you want to purchase at a later date. Smartika stores these products in your Wishlist and you can view them anytime you login to your Smartika Account which you will be able to create in the Check Out page.

If you wish to add a product to your Wishlist, just visit the Shop, click on the product you want to add to Wishlist, scroll down to the bottom of that product’s description, and right below the Add To Cart Button, you will see an Add To Wishlist option. Press that and the product will be added to your Wishlist and you can purchase it at a later date. You can view your Wishlist anytime at the Wishlist page in the menu.
